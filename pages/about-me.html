Hi, I'm Cosmo, the main driving force behind Atlas_Cosmo. Atlas_Cosmo is the collective name for all the work I do as a composer and sound designer, creating electronic music for video games. I was raised as a musician, its something I've done my whole life and something I'd love to continue to do.
I'm proficient in a few different instruments and have been learning the piano since I was 8. I have alot of experience in software such as Logic Pro, Fmod and Wwise. And have worked with other other people on multiple small projects. Most of my education comes from a 2 year course I'm currently doing at The University Center at Wakefield college.
